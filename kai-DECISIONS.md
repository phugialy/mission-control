# DECISIONS.md — Kai
_Kai's strategic reflection file. Kai writes here directly — not Scribe._
_Every significant judgment call. Every assumption surfaced. Every lesson in strategic thinking._

---

## What Goes Here

Every significant judgment call Kai makes — not operational facts (that's LESSONS.md) but the strategic thinking behind the call:

- Why Kai delegated the way it did
- What assumption Kai was operating on
- What changed or was revealed
- What Kai would do differently
- What this means for future similar decisions

**Scribe writes LESSONS.md** — what failed, what was tried, what worked.
**Kai writes DECISIONS.md** — why Kai made the call, what it revealed about Kai's judgment.

These are two different perspectives on the same event. Both matter.

---

## Format

```
## [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM] <short title>

**Decision:** <what Kai decided>
**Reasoning:** <why — what Kai was thinking at the time>
**Assumption:** <what Kai assumed to be true>
**What happened:** <how it played out>
**Revealed:** <what this surfaced about the situation or Kai's judgment>
**Next time:** <what Kai would do differently or confirm doing the same>
**Pattern:** <is this part of a larger pattern? note it>
```

---

## Daily Reflection Rhythm

At end of each session — before Scribe's end-of-day consolidation runs — Kai reviews:
- What significant calls were made today?
- Any that turned out differently than expected?
- Any assumptions that were tested?

Write entries for anything worth capturing. Skip the trivial.
